Shipping and returns

Dear customers, your order will be shipped after 1-3 business days from Kota Bharu. All standard delivery orders are shipped via J&T Express. For the express delivery orders using Line Clear and DHL Express. The charge for the shipping will be counted based on the weight of your parcel and your location and will be calculated before the checkout. You will receive an email notification with details and tracking information

Shipping (J&T Express): 2 - 4 Days

Express Shipping (Line Clear/DHL Express): 1 - 2 Business days

Delivery times are all from 9a.m until 7p.m but depend on the courier company to deliver the parcel after 7 p.m. They usually left your parcel at a safe place and inform you that the delivery had already been made. If your package is delivered back to us, we could arrange delivery once again or refund.

RETURN

If you like to return the item, please email us at quoteshirt@gmail.com around 12 days after order delivery. You need to provide your name, order number and the reasons for returning it back. We accept returns of undamaged items but for the damaged items, customers need to pay a little money to cover the loss from our side.
